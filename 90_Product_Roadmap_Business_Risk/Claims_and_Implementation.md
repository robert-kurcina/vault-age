# Claims and Implementation — Product Roadmap, Business Risk, and MVP Narrowing

## Claims

- The first product must be narrower than the platform vision.
- Games and serious play precede regulated professional domains.
- Agent Service is a later extension.

## Implementation steps

1. Define MVP buyer and use case.
2. Build one playable adventure.
3. Add authoring package format.
4. Add one rules corpus.
5. Run baseline benchmarks.
6. Expand to serious play after runtime proof.

## Risks and controls

- Risk: platform sprawl. Control: MVP boundaries.
- Risk: unclear buyer. Control: pick first market.
- Risk: overclaiming. Control: benchmark and stage claims.
