*I am operating from the mind-set that anything involving rules is a game. This includes simple things like tic-tac-toe, but it also includes regulatory fields like health and law.* 

The Amazing Game Engine (AGE) is an AI-driven platform designed to **ensure high-fidelity behavior and narrative intent across interactive media**. 

At its core, AGE anchors LLMs to reliably interpret and act within structured worlds, giving content creators the ability to define authentic Roles—ranging from actors and directors to business, legal, and medical agents—that behave consistently and credibly. 

By extending into **Roles-as-a-Service**, the platform captures, evaluates, and grades Role authenticity, enabling modular, reproducible, and auditable interactions. 

AGE is both a creative sandbox and a trust framework, bridging game mechanics, mixed-media storytelling, and domain-specific services, ultimately evolving into a **platform and certification standard** that guarantees controlled, high-quality experiences in interactive media and beyond.

## The Roadmap

* Start with an interactive game engine.

* Continue as a service.

* Establish Roles as a Service (RaaS).

* Expand to a platform

* Establish a certification standard.
